About Us

Astro-Enthusiasts is a platform to connect anyone and everyone who has the fascination for the stars, the galaxies and our universe. For all those interested in astronomy, including our journal readers and authors, educators, and those working in industry.

Our Objectives

  • Develop scientific temper.
  • Study the vastness of the universe
  • Develop the skills to use telescope.
  • Identify various celestial bodies.
  • Understand the concepts of astronomy.
  • Encourage Amateur Astronomers.
